View and manage newsletter subscribers from your website.

## Prerequisites

- **Paid subscription** (not available on free plan)
- A newsletter/signup form on your website

## How to access

Navigate to **Dashboard → [Your Website] → Subscribers**.

## Viewing subscribers

You’ll see:

- Subscriber email addresses
- Subscription date for each subscriber
- Total subscriber count

If you have no subscribers yet, you’ll see **“No subscribers yet.”**

## Exporting subscribers

1. Click **Export Subscribers** (top right).
2. A CSV downloads automatically (for example: `website_subscribers.csv`).
3. Open it in Excel/Google Sheets to import, analyze, or back up your list.

## Free plan behavior

On the free plan, you may see a prompt like **“Upgrade to a Paid plan to view and edit settings.”**

## Collecting subscribers (newsletter form)

To collect subscribers:

1. Go to the website editor.
2. Add a section that includes a newsletter signup form.
3. Ensure the form includes an email input and is connected to the newsletter feature.
